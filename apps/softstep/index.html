Soft Step

Slow Jogging Timer & Metronome 超慢跑計時節拍器 超慢跑计时节拍器 スロージョギング タイマー＆メトロノーム 슬로우 조깅 타이머 & 메트로놈

Soft Step is an iOS app designed specifically for slow jogging. Combining a timer, metronome, and health data tracking, it helps you effortlessly maintain healthy workout habits. Whether you're a beginner or a fitness enthusiast looking to track your data, Soft Step has you covered.
