Last Updated: February 6, 2026

Thank you for using Soft Step (Slow Jogging) app. We value your privacy, and this policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Important Notice

Soft Step is a privacy-focused fitness tracking app. Your workout data and personal information are primarily stored on your device, and we do not transmit your personal data to our servers.

1. Data Collection

1.1 Locally Stored Data

The following data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to any server:

Data Type Description
Workout Records Workout date, time, duration, goal completion status
Workout Statistics Steps, calories burned, distance (some are estimated values)
Body Information Height, weight, age, gender (used for accurate calorie calculation)
App Preferences Language, appearance mode, metronome settings, reminder time

1.2 Apple HealthKit Data

With your explicit authorization, this app reads and writes the following health data:

Data Read (Requires Your Authorization)

  • Step Count
  • Heart Rate
  • Active Energy Burned
  • Walking + Running Distance
  • Height
  • Body Mass
  • Date of Birth
  • Biological Sex

Data Written (Requires Your Authorization)

  • Workout - Sync your slow jogging workouts to Apple Health app

1.3 Data We Do Not Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, phone number, address, email)
  • Location information (this app does not use GPS)
  • Usage behavior analytics data
  • Account or password information

2. Data Usage

Data Type Purpose
Workout Records Track your workout progress, display statistical reports
Body Data Accurately calculate calorie consumption and estimate workout distance
HealthKit Data Bidirectional sync of workout records with Apple Health app
Preferences Provide personalized user experience

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Google AdMob

This app uses Google AdMob to provide advertising services. Google may collect the following information:

  • Device identifiers
  • Advertising identifier (IDFA)
  • Usage analytics and performance data

This data is used to provide relevant advertising content and improve advertising services.

How to opt out of personalized ads: You can adjust your advertising preferences in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3.2 Apple HealthKit

HealthKit data is securely managed by Apple and follows Apple's privacy policy.

Apple Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All workout data is stored only on your device
  • We do not operate any servers; we do not collect or transmit your personal data
  • Deleting the app will permanently remove all local data
  • HealthKit data is encrypted and protected by Apple

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties.

6. Your Rights

You can exercise the following rights at any time:

  • View Data: View all workout records and statistics within the app
  • Delete Data: Remove all local data by deleting the app
  • Revoke HealthKit Authorization: Manage in iOS Settings > Health > Data Access & Devices
  • Opt Out of Personalized Ads: Adjust advertising preferences in device settings

7. Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. International Users

Regardless of your location, your personal data is stored only on your device and is not transferred to any servers across borders. This app complies with the data protection requirements of the following regulations:

  • European Union General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Japan Act on the Protection of Personal Information (APPI)
  • Korea Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA)

9. Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Significant changes will be notified in the app update notes. We recommend that you review this policy periodically.

10. Contact Us
